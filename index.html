Jeepney Simulator: The game changer of our future generation

We used computers to write and compute data back in the days. But as technology evolves, every generation tries to use their creativity to explore the unknown. Video games has a huge impact to every youth who used its childhood to experience playing certain games that will surely leave a mark.

Jeepney Simulator is created by two filipino students from Davao. Joshua Renzie A. Bicoy is the lead developer, while Alvinn Vann V. Arapoc is the 3D artist of the game under Spacezero Interactive. You may think of why did they create this game. As per Joshua, it is on his mind for quite some time "as a joke". But as the time goes by, Alvin had the same idea and they made it into a reality. In their spare time, they usually code it and work hard for it to develop. They made a prototype immediately. Since Joshua's experience involved in 2D games, it was really a challenge for him to create this one.

They almost got their hardwork project to lose because of some strange unity error. When Alvin entered to give a helping hand, the project continued to grow as where it is today. Video games like this has its own ups and down and Jeepney Simulator is no exception from it. It is proven that the determination that Joshua and Alvin has it's own fruit of labor. Their passion for this game made them successful in finishing their demo in time.

    So how does this game work?
  • You get the vehicle at your in-game father's garage.
  • Drive it to every location where you can fetch your passenger.
  • Get the passengers' money.
  • Give the passenger their change (if they have any).
  • Drop the passenger to their preferred location.
  • After your shift, get back to the garage and go to your house.
  • You can only end your day once you decided click the bed.

    Feature of this game:
  • Traffic System- It has a stop light and it it designed to experince reality.
  • Customization: You can change the color of your Jeepney. Also you can upgrade the fuel capacity
  • Manage your Family: You could be able to change the statistic of your family.
    (It could be their happiness, health and other stats included here)
  • Hiraya Corporation: Uncover the secrets of Hiraya Corporation that makes the game more exciting
  • Immersive Experience:You could literally feel how to become a jeepney driver just by sitting in front
    of your computers
